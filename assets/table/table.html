ANIMALS IN THE TABLE

The Red Book of Kazakhstan recalls the consequences of the rapid development of the country's natural resources. Man violates ecosystems that have been created for centuries. Therefore, many species of fauna have already disappeared, and the animals of the Red Book of Kazakhstan require protection. Recently , new representatives have been added to itThe Red Book is a list of rare and endangered animals, plants and fungi in Kazakhstan. It has 3 editions, the last of which was published in 1999.
